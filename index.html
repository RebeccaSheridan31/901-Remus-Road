About the scheme

901 Remus Road is a scheme providing 280 co-living homes and 44 affordable homes at 100% social rent. The plans also include improvements to the area around the site — new trees, landscaping, children's play areas and a social co-working café open to the whole community.

Here are some key points you could consider including:
Location
This site is an underutilised car park which is limited in what it can offer to the local community. It's important that we use brownfield sites like this for housing so that we can protect green spaces from being built on.
Co-living Homes
The plans will deliver 280 new, high-quality co-living homes with an all-inclusive rental model priced below typical Build to Rent schemes — ideal for young professionals and key workers who have been hit hard by the housing crisis.
Affordable Homes
Tower Hamlets is facing an acute housing crisis. 901 Remus Road will provide 44 homes, including family-sized homes, at 100% social rent — easing pressure on housing waiting lists and giving families the homes they need.
Green Space
The plans include communal green space at the heart of the development, with play space for children, new trees and planting — improving the neighbourhood for everyone.
Community
A social co-working cafe open to the whole community will give people in Fish Island a new place to come together, strengthening the local neighbourhood.
